What is DePIN?

DePIN stands for Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Networks. Instead of big companies owning infrastructure like wireless networks, storage, sensors, or compute power, DePIN lets everyday people contribute hardware and get rewarded with tokens. It’s a way to build real‑world infrastructure using blockchain incentives.
