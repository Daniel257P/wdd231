Discover Jarrell

Local History

  • Founded in the late 1800s as a railroad community
  • Named after real estate developer E.J. Jarrell
  • Historic ties to agriculture and ranching
  • Known for strong community resilience and growth

Current Demographics

  • Population: ~5,151 (2026 projection)
  • Median Age: 34.3
  • Rapid growth: +21% to +28% annually
  • Median Household Income: $101,653
  • Racial/Ethnic Composition: 48% White, 36% Hispanic, 12% Black
