About Jarrell

Jarrell is a fast‑growing community in Central Texas, located along the I‑35 corridor between Austin and Waco.

What was once a small rural stop has transformed into a vibrant town attracting families, entrepreneurs, and new developments every year. The community blends small‑town charm with modern conveniences, offering a peaceful lifestyle while staying connected to major Texas cities. Jarrell is known for its welcoming atmosphere, strong local pride, and a business‑friendly environment supported by the Chamber of Commerce. With new neighborhoods, parks, schools, and commercial projects emerging, Jarrell continues to evolve while preserving the values that make it special.

Why Live in Jarrell?

  • Safe and friendly neighborhoods
  • Growing business opportunities
  • Affordable living
  • Great schools and parks
